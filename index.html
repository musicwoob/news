Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DistroKid

How quickly is my music delivered?

DistroKid delivers music faster than any other service. Most stores receive your music within 1-3 business days. Some stores like Spotify and Apple Music can take 1-7 business days. TikTok is usually within 48 hours.

How do I get paid?

DistroKid sends you 100% of royalties earned from streaming and downloads. Payments are made automatically each month via PayPal, wire transfer, or check once you've earned at least $10.

Can I release cover songs?

Yes! DistroKid can obtain mechanical licenses for cover songs on your behalf. This is included with all plans at no extra cost. We handle all paperwork and payments to the original songwriters.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your DistroKid subscription, your music will remain in stores. However, you won't be able to upload new music or access certain features. You'll continue to receive royalties for existing releases.
